Microsoft Teams Help

What is Microsoft Teams?

Microsoft Teams is a messaging app that uses chat instead of email and channels instead of file folders. It is a workspace for you and your team to securely edits files at the same time and where all conversations, meetings, files, and notes can be accessed by everyone, all in one place no matter where you are.

Why do we use it?

We use it to communicate with our faculty, staff, and students over the web for web based learning in order to comply with social distancing factors and be able to offer students and faculty a safe way to communicate effectively.

What are teams?

Teams are made up of a select group of people within an organization coming together to get tasks done like projects, meetings, and etc.

What is a team channel?

  1. Channels are were you have meetings, work on files with others, and have conversations
  2. At the top of the channel you will find your tabs where your files and apps will be that the team is using
  3. Teams are made up of 2 types of channels, the standard one that is visible to everyone and private ones. Each channel is created around a topic
  4. When a channel is private that means you have added specific people you would want to have focused and private conversation with.
  5. To create a new channel, right beside your teams name click the three dots and in the drop down menu click on add a channel
  6. Create a channel name and maybe put down a description, also decide who will be on this channel
  7. If your team has more than one set of channels then you can pin the most important ones to the top by hovering over the channel then going to and clicking the three dots that will appear and in the drop down menu look for and click on "pin"
  8. To unpin a channel from the top, click the three dots beside it and in the drop down menu click on unpin.

How do I make someone else the host?

If you are the current host of a team meeting, you have the option to make someone else the host. While in a meeting find the person's name and click the three dots that will appear. In the drop down menu find and click make a host. And they will be the host

What apps can my team use?

Over in the left side task bar when you click on the three dots you will be given a list of all the apps your team can use. Once you find one you like click it and it will appear. You can also add apps in the tab area by clicking the plus sign Once it is added your team members will be able to use it as well.

How do I make sure my microphone is working?

  1. To make sure your microphone is working, in the tab bar on your desktop right click on teams and then click on Settings
  2. In the settings find Devices and click it
  3. If you have more than one mic connected make sure you are using the correct one
  4. Under that click on "make a test call"
  5. Then follow the team echo prompts directions. After you will get your test call results

How do I put a virtual background on in Teams?

*Disclaimer: If your virtual backgrounds does not work for you, then that means your computers processor and or graphics card is not compatible with this feature.

  1. When you are on a teams call, click the three dots on the meeting settings bar called "More Action"
  2. Select "Apply background effects"
  3. Choose your background from the background setting pane
    1. If you want a custom background make sure that it has a file size of no less that 1920 x 1080
    2. If you download an image and it does not work then that is because the file size does not work with teams.
  4. Either preview or select apply to enable the background
